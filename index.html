What We Do
We don't just show data.
We change behaviour.

Green Beacons designs and deploys AI-integrated participant visibility systems for workforce development programmes — giving programme managers real-time insight into every individual, across every organisation.

We set it up, train your overseers or deploy our own trained coordinators, and hand you a living system that replaces manual reporting with real-time progress visibility across all your partner organisations.

Most programmes track activity. Very few track behaviour. That gap is where performance is lost.

The System
Three pillars.
One living system.
01

Tracking

Every participant has their own page, updated in real time. Programme managers can move from high-level org view to individual activity in three clicks — without waiting for a monthly report.

3-click visibility
02

Intelligence

Claude AI reads the live system and generates structured performance reports, flags inactive participants, and delivers personalised recommendations per person — based on exactly where they are in their journey.

Agentic AI
03

Visibility

Participants access their own pages via a shared link on their phones, at no cost. They update their own progress and can see what others in their organisation are doing, creating natural accountability. Coordinators and managers work in the same system.
